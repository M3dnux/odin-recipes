Homemade Pie Crust

This homemade pie crust is my personal favorite and is made using a food processor, which makes cutting the butter into the flour very simple. You can make a double batch of this and freeze the individually wrapped dough for future pie-related adventures.

Ingredients

These are the five ingredients you’ll need to make this extra easy homemade pie crust recipe:

  • Flour: This easy homemade pie crust recipe starts with all-purpose flour.
  • Butter: You’ll need two sticks of unsalted butter, chilled and cut into pieces.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor and promotes a tender crust.
  • Ice water: Make sure your water is ice cold for the flakiest results.
  • Apple cider vinegar: Vinegar inhibits gluten production, preventing a tough crust.

Steps

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make this homemade pie crust:

  1. Pulse the first three ingredients in a food processor.
  2. Stir the water and vinegar together and pulse.
  3. Pour in the remaining water-vinegar mixture and pulse.
  4. Turn the dough out and pat it into two discs.
  5. Wrap each disc in plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to use.
